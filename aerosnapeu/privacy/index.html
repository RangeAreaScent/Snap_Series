Aero Snap (EU) · Legal

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-03

Aero Snap (EU) is designed to be fully offline and tracking-free. This page exists because the App Store and the Apple Developer guidelines require every app to publish a privacy policy, even one as minimal as this.

TL;DR

  • Aero Snap (EU) does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on any server.
  • Aero Snap (EU) does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDKs of any kind.
  • Aero Snap (EU) does not require an account and never asks for one.
  • All your data — favourites, collections, notes — stays on your device.

What runs on your device

The app uses local storage on your device for:

StoredPurposeWhere
Favourited regulations Quick access to EASA Easy Access Rules sections you mark with a star. SwiftData
Collections Per-registration / per-fleet folders for compliance tracking. SwiftData
Per-regulation notes Your free-text annotations on a given section. SwiftData
Per-item compliance status Optional checkmark + date on each regulation in a folder, plus free-text shop notes. SwiftData
App preferences Theme, copy format, citation style, haptic toggle. UserDefaults

This data is never sent off the device. There is no sync, no cloud backup managed by the app, and no telemetry.

What does NOT happen

  • No analytics framework (no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, no Firebase, no App Analytics opt-ins, no Plausible, nothing).
  • No advertising SDKs.
  • No tracking identifiers (IDFA, ad ID, etc.) requested or used.
  • No crash-reporting SDK that uploads data (we rely on Apple's anonymous opt-in crash reports only, which Apple — not us — manages).
  • No remote configuration, A/B testing, or feature flags.
  • No third-party fonts, web requests, or content-delivery calls.
  • No network access requested outside of the optional in-app purchase flow (Apple StoreKit) and the user-initiated "Open on EASA" / "Open on EUR-Lex" / "ad.easa.europa.eu" links — those open in Safari and are governed by Safari's privacy settings, not ours.

GDPR — EU users

Because this app targets EU aviation professionals, the General Data Protection Regulation applies. Aero Snap (EU) is GDPR-compliant by design: we are not a data controller or data processor for any personal data, because the app does not collect, process, or transmit any personal data. Your device's filesystem is the sole location of any data you generate inside the app. You have full control to delete it at any time by deleting the app.

In-app purchase (optional supporter unlock)

If you choose to support the app with the optional one-time purchase ("Buy me a coffee" / Supporter), iOS uses Apple StoreKit. Apple receives the standard purchase transaction; we receive only a confirmation that the purchase happened. We never see your Apple ID, payment method, or billing address.

The purchase is entirely optional. The core app — search, favourites, collections, notes, Parts browser, TCDS catalog, Part-66 Reference, PDF/CSV export — is fully free with no time limits or feature locks. The only thing the purchase unlocks is the premium theme set + alternate app icons.

Data you export

The PDF and CSV export features write files to your device's temp directory and hand them to the system share sheet. From there, you decide who sees the file (Mail, AirDrop, your filesystem, your organisation's quality-management software). Aero Snap (EU) does not upload exports anywhere.

Bundled dataset attribution

Aero Snap (EU) bundles a snapshot of the following EASA + EU public material for offline lookup:

  • EASA Easy Access Rules — Continuing Airworthiness (Part-M / 145 / 66 / 147 / CAMO / CAO / ML / Part-T) and Initial Airworthiness (Part-21), extracted from the EASA Document Library XML.
  • EASA Type Certificate Data Sheets — curated metadata subset, extracted from the EASA TCDS catalog.
  • Part-66 licence reference — categories and modules from Annex III of Regulation (EU) 1321/2014.
  • Workflow topic index — hand-curated cross-references between common maintenance workflows and the EASA rule sections that govern them.

All bundled documents are reused under Commission Decision 2011/833/EU (free reuse of European Commission documents). The "Open on EASA" links inside the app point at the authoritative EASA source for each document; the bundled snapshot is provided for fast offline lookup, not as a substitute for the controlled document.

EASA Airworthiness Directives are not bundled. The app directs users to ad.easa.europa.eu for real-time AD compliance.

Children's privacy

Aero Snap (EU) is designed for Part-66 certifying staff, Part-145 organisation Quality Managers, Part-CAMO airworthiness reviewers, and Part-147 maintenance-training instructors. It is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.
