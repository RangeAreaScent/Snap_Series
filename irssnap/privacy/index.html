IRS Snap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

IRS Snap does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

  • All IRS data — Internal Revenue Code (26 USC), Treasury Regulations (26 CFR), IRS Forms and Publications — is bundled locally on your device. The dataset is in the public domain (uscode.house.gov, ecfr.gov, irs.gov).
  • Favorites, collections, notes, and recently-viewed items are stored only on your device — SwiftData on iOS, plain JSON files on macOS and Windows.
  • No analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs.
  • The only network requests are:
    • iOS: Apple's StoreKit for the optional one-time in-app purchase, handled entirely by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policy.
    • macOS/Windows: Lemon Squeezy license activation and validation (if you choose to enter a license key). Only the license key + a generated install name are sent. No personal information.
    • Tapping "Open PDF" on a Form/Publication detail opens the associated PDF on irs.gov in your default browser; that request is made by the browser, not by IRS Snap.
  • IRS Snap is for reference only. Always verify against current IRS guidance and consult a qualified tax professional for filing or planning decisions.
