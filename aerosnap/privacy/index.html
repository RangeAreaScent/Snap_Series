Aero Snap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Aero Snap does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app is offline-first and tracking-free.

What runs on your device

  • All Airworthiness Directive, 14 CFR, TCDS, and Advisory Circular data is bundled locally on your device — no network lookup for any search or detail view.
  • Favorited ADs, My Aircraft folders, per-AD notes, and per-aircraft compliance status are stored only on your device (SwiftData).
  • App preferences (theme, copy format, Bluebook citation toggle, haptic toggle) are stored only on your device (UserDefaults).
  • No analytics framework, no advertising SDKs, no tracking identifiers, no third-party SDKs of any kind.

Network requests

The app makes no automatic network requests. The only outgoing traffic is initiated by you:

  • "Open on Federal Register" / "Official PDF" / TCDS document links — these open in Safari on tap. Whatever happens after that is governed by Safari's privacy settings, not ours.
  • Optional one-time supporter purchase ("Buy me a coffee") uses Apple StoreKit. Apple handles the transaction entirely; we receive only a confirmation that the purchase happened. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

The core app — search across all five modes, favorites, aircraft collections, notes, 14 CFR browser, TCDS catalog, PDF/CSV export — is fully free with no time limits or feature locks. The supporter purchase only unlocks the premium theme set.

Exports

PDF and CSV exports are written to your device's temp directory and handed to the system share sheet. From there, you decide who sees the file (Mail, AirDrop, your filesystem, your shop's fleet-management software). Aero Snap never uploads exports anywhere.

Bundled dataset attribution

Aero Snap bundles a snapshot of:

  • FAA Airworthiness Directives (Federal Register API)
  • 14 CFR — Federal Aviation Regulations (eCFR XML API)
  • Type Certificate Data Sheets (curated subset of FAA TCDS PDFs)
  • Advisory Circulars (FAA AC index)
All four datasets are works of the United States Federal Government and are in the public domain under 17 U.S.C. § 105. The bundled snapshot is provided for fast offline lookup, not as a substitute for the authoritative controlled document. The "Open on Federal Register" and "Official PDF" links inside the app point at the canonical federal source for each document.

Children's privacy

Aero Snap is designed for A&P mechanics, IAs, aircraft owners, and aviation maintenance professionals. It is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.
