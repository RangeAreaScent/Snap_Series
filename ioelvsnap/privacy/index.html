IOELV Snap (EU) · Legal

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-22

IOELV Snap (EU) — the iOS app and the Mac / Windows desktop app — is designed to be fully offline and tracking-free. This page exists because the App Store, the Mac App Store, the Microsoft Store, and Apple / Google / Microsoft developer guidelines all require every app to publish a privacy policy, even one as minimal as this.

TL;DR

  • IOELV Snap (EU) does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on any server.
  • IOELV Snap (EU) does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDKs of any kind.
  • IOELV Snap (EU) does not require an account and never asks for one.
  • All your data — favorites, collections, notes, settings — stays on your device.

What runs on your device

The app uses local storage on your device for:

StoredPurposeWhere
Favorited substances Quick access to substances you mark with a star — your personal shortlist. iOS: SwiftData · Desktop: localStorage
Collections Named lists of substances ("Lab B-3 inventory", "Q4 audit scope") with per-substance notes. iOS: SwiftData · Desktop: localStorage
Per-substance notes Your free-text annotations on a given substance ("BBLV checked 2026-Q2", "ref: SOP-04 rev 3"). iOS: SwiftData · Desktop: localStorage
TWA calculator / SDS draft state In-progress measurements + SDS template content while you work. Desktop: localStorage
App preferences Theme, font size, copy format, app icon, haptic toggle. iOS: UserDefaults · Desktop: localStorage

This data is never sent off the device. There is no sync, no cloud backup managed by the app, and no telemetry.

What does NOT happen

  • No analytics framework (no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, no Firebase, no App Analytics opt-ins, no Plausible, nothing).
  • No advertising SDKs.
  • No tracking identifiers (IDFA, ad ID, etc.) requested or used.
  • No crash-reporting SDK that uploads data (we rely on Apple's anonymous opt-in crash reports only, which Apple — not us — manages).
  • No remote configuration, A/B testing, or feature flags.
  • No third-party fonts, web requests, or content-delivery calls.
  • No network access requested outside of (a) the optional in-app purchase / license activation flow and (b) the user-initiated external links described below — those open in your default browser and are governed by your browser's privacy settings, not ours.

GDPR — EU users

Because this app targets EU occupational-safety professionals working under EU OSH law, the General Data Protection Regulation applies directly. IOELV Snap (EU) is GDPR-compliant by design: we are not a data controller or data processor for any personal data, because the app does not collect, process, or transmit any personal data. Your device's filesystem is the sole location of any data you generate inside the app. You have full control to delete it at any time by deleting the app.

External links — opened in your browser

Several screens (substance detail, no-results fallback, Help menu on desktop) surface link-only buttons to external authoritative sources. These open in your default browser on tap. We do not pre-fetch, mirror, proxy, or instrument these links — they go directly from your browser to the destination, and we never see whether you tapped them.

In-app purchase / license activation (optional)

If you choose to support the app with the optional one-time purchase ("Buy me a coffee" / Supporter), the platform handles payment:

  • iOS: Apple StoreKit. Apple receives the standard purchase transaction; we receive only a confirmation that the purchase happened. We never see your Apple ID, payment method, or billing address.
  • Mac / Windows desktop: Lemon Squeezy license key. The license-activation API call sends only the license key string and a stable per-install instance name ("IOELV Snap (EU) Desktop"); no personal information.

The purchase is entirely optional. The core app — search, favorites, collections, notes, Compare, TWA Calculator, Unit Converter, SDS draft, PDF/CSV export, ECHA fallback — is fully free with no time limits or feature locks. The only thing the purchase unlocks is the premium theme set + alternate app icons.

Data you export

The PDF and CSV export features (Compare, Collection, TWA result, SDS draft) write files to a location you choose via the system file-save dialog. From there, you decide who sees the file (Mail, AirDrop, your filesystem, your organisation's quality-management software). IOELV Snap (EU) does not upload exports anywhere.

Bundled dataset attribution

IOELV Snap (EU) bundles a snapshot of the following EU public material for offline lookup (187 substances · 0.49 MB SQLite + FTS5):

  • EUR-Lex IOELV / BOELV Directives — Council Directives 91/322/EEC, 2000/39/EC, 2006/15/EC, 2009/161/EU, 2017/164/EU and CMRD 2004/37/EC + amendments (2017/2398/EU, 2019/130/EU, 2019/983/EU, 2022/431/EU, 2024/869/EU).
  • CLP Annex VI Harmonised Classification — CMR category + H-statement assignments per Regulation (EC) 1272/2008 Annex VI Table 3.
  • REACH Annex XVII restriction entries — Regulation (EC) 1907/2006 Annex XVII.
  • ECHA RAC scientific opinion URLs — link-only; PDF bodies are NOT bundled.
  • 27 Member State OEL authority URLs — link-only; national OEL bodies are NOT bundled.
  • Multilingual substance synonyms — EN · DE · FR · IT · ES.

All bundled EU documents are re-used under the EU Open Data Directive 2019/1024 and, where applicable, Commission Decision 2011/833/EU (free re-use of European Commission documents).

What is deliberately NOT bundled. Commercial Member State publications (DFG MAK Collection body text, INRS Editions Fiches Toxicologiques, Sdu / Wolters Kluwer / Wiley-VCH body content) and non-EU OEL frameworks (ACGIH TLV values) are excluded by design — the app links to the public authority pages instead, in compliance with their licenses.

Intended users

IOELV Snap (EU) is designed for EU industrial hygienists, EHS leads, lab safety officers, occupational physicians, and compliance auditors working under EU OSH law. It is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Independence

This app is independent and not affiliated with ECHA, the European Commission, or any Member State authority. All trademarks belong to their respective owners. The bundled snapshot is provided for fast offline lookup, not as a substitute for the controlled official documents — for any binding interpretation, always consult the EUR-Lex authoritative text and your national authority.
