LOINC Snap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

LOINC Snap does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app is offline-first and tracking-free.

What runs on your device

  • All LOINC data is bundled locally on your device — no network lookup.
  • Favorites, collections, per-code notes, and recent searches are stored only on your device (SwiftData on iOS, local SQLite on desktop).
  • App preferences (theme, font size, copy format) are stored only on your device.
  • No analytics framework, no advertising SDKs, no tracking identifiers, no third-party SDKs of any kind.

The one network request

The only network request the app may make is for the optional one-time supporter purchase ("Buy me a coffee"):

  • iOS uses Apple StoreKit. Apple handles the transaction entirely; we receive only a confirmation. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Desktop uses Lemon Squeezy for checkout + license validation. Subject to Lemon Squeezy's Privacy Policy. We receive only your license key and checkout email, used solely to validate the purchase on a second device. We don't add you to any mailing list.

The core app — search, favorites, collections, notes, export — is fully free with no time limits or feature locks.

Exports

PDF and CSV exports are written to your device's temp directory and handed to the system share sheet. From there, you decide who sees the file. LOINC Snap never uploads exports anywhere.
